About DeBunk'D

Welcome to DeBunk'D! In today's digital age, the spread of misinformation and disinformation poses a significant threat to informed decision-making and a healthy society. DeBunk'D is designed to be your personal assistant in navigating this complex information landscape.

Our mission is to empower users like you to critically evaluate the news, identify false narratives, and understand the various perspectives surrounding important issues. We achieve this by providing:

  • AI-Powered Fact-Checking: Summarize articles, highlight key findings, present multiple perspectives, and cite verified sources using advanced AI.
  • Interactive Learning: A media literacy chatbot and informative articles with quizzes to enhance your critical thinking skills.
  • Personalized Experience: Save articles for later reference and track your learning progress.

We believe that by fostering media literacy, we can contribute to a more informed and resilient community. Join us in the fight against misinformation!
